Name: Anti-Matter Disposal Unit (AMDU)

The AMDU uses .5mg to disintegrate Hazardous Materials like Pyronium or Cryonium
Safety Protocols:
    Once Materials Placed inside:
        Exit the Chamber
        Turn Key
        Press Button with label "Dispose"

The AMDU has never malfunctioned in its history, it was created 15Y ago
Required access level to activate: 3A (Scientist)
